What is it?

ToonHQ+ helps improve your ToontownRewritten experience by enhancing the ToonHQ site with additional information/features that build upon the already great tools that ToonHQ provides.
 

How does it work?

ToonHQ+ consists of a bookmarklet that temporarily adds javascript to the ToonHQ webpages within your browser, which turns on the additional features. Because there is no plugin involved, there is no need to make modifications to your browser or do any other installation -- you simply create a new ToonHQ+ bookmark in your browser, and click on it when you are on ToonHQ to enable the added features. If you don't want to create a bookmark, you can even just cut and paste a command into your browser's address bar while you are on the ToonHQ site, and ToonHQ+ will be enabled!

ToonHQ+ Activation Instructions

In order to activate the ToonHQ+ you must run the ToonHQ+ javascript bookmarklet code while you are on the ToonHQ site. There are two options for doing this:

  • Option 1:  Create a bookmark in your browser, and click on it when you are on the ToonHQ site. To do this:
    • Make sure the Bookmarks or Favorites bar is open and visible in your browser
    • Click and drag this link: ToonHQ+ to your Bookmarks/Favorites bar (or right click and select "Add link to Bookmarks"), and the bookmark will be created. You may rename the bookmark if you wish
    • Once the bookmark is created, click on it whenever the ToonHQ site is loaded (or reloaded) to activate ToonHQ+. You can either click on the bookmark from the Bookmarks/Favorites bar, or from the Menu (some versions of Edge require clicking on it from the Favorites bar)
  • Option 2:  Go to the ToonHQ site, and paste the javasscript bookmarklet code into you browser address bar:
    • Clear out all text in the address bar
    • type "javascript:" (no quotes) in the address bar, and after "javascript" cut and paste the following:
      • (function(){var j=document.createElement('script');j.setAttribute('src','https://earlofcloves.github.io/THQ_GroupPlus/THQ_GroupPlus.js');document.body.appendChild(j)}())
    • The address bar should now begin with "javascript: (function() (var j=...". Hit enter, and ToonHQ+ should be activated.

Each time you open or reopen the ToonHQ site tab/window, you will need either click on the bookmark or rerun the command from Option 2.
